ABOUT

Himesh Chauhan

Born on 29.Sep.2005 in Gurugram Haryana. He is self-taught beginner artist. He started his journey with chalk micro sculpture crafting. He participated in Group art exhibition: - ARTENET group art exhibition in gurgaon. - RANGSAAZ 2 at National Crafts Museum & Hastkala Academy, Delhi. He also participated in many Online & Offline exhibition. He feels that Art is the best form of meditation for artists and art lovers. He is doing work on Mythology art & Painting. He also done 3 art show at his school. He started His Journey 3 years ago.
